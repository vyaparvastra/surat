Frequently Asked Questions

SuratKhoobSurat Quiz & History App

What is SuratKhoobSurat application?

SuratKhoobSurat is a quiz-based application designed to explore Surat’s history, culture, and famous places in a fun way.

How does the quiz work?

Users can answer multiple-choice questions based on Surat history and instantly see their results.

Is the application free?

Yes, the application is completely free for all users.

What topics are included?

Topics include Surat’s historical places, landmarks, culture, and interesting facts.

Can I track my results?

Yes, you can view your quiz results and performance summary.

How can I contact support?

You can contact us via email at info@suratkhoobsurat.in
